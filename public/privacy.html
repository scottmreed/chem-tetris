Privacy Policy

ChemIllusion: IUPAC Rain. Last updated March 7, 2026.

This Privacy Policy explains how ChemIllusion: IUPAC Rain handles information when you launch the Discord activity at app.mechanismsolver.org.

Information We Process

  • Your Discord user ID
  • Your Discord username or global display name
  • Your Discord avatar hash, if available
  • Temporary game-session data such as lobby membership, score, and ready state
  • Standard hosting and request logs generated by Discord and Cloudflare

How We Use Information

  • Authenticate you inside the Discord Embedded App SDK
  • Show your name and avatar in the lobby, gameplay UI, and score displays
  • Run single-player and multiplayer game sessions
  • Exchange Discord OAuth authorization codes for access tokens
  • Diagnose crashes, auth failures, and service availability issues

Storage and Retention

The production Pages deployment does not maintain a long-term user database for this activity. Game state in the current production build is held in memory for the active browser tab. OAuth codes are exchanged server-side and are not intentionally stored by the application after the request completes.

Sharing

The activity relies on Discord for identity and avatar delivery, and on Cloudflare Pages/Functions for hosting and token exchange. Those providers may process request metadata according to their own policies.

Children

This activity is intended for Discord users who are permitted to use Discord under Discord’s own terms and age requirements.

Your Choices

If you do not want the activity to access your Discord identity information, do not launch or authorize the activity. You can also revoke app access from your Discord settings.

Changes

This policy may be updated as the activity changes. Updates will be published on this page with a revised effective date.

Contact

For questions about this policy, open an issue at github.com/scottmreed/chem-tetris/issues.
